PRICING AND SALON CUSTOMS


How Our Tiered Level Based Pricing works!

Student Stylist: We believe in growing our industry which is why we team up with local Cosmetology Schools and eager students wanting to learn. These stylists complete our 6 month training program and perform services on live models to complete each area of training. These stylists work closely with Level 5-6 stylists.

Level 1 Stylist: These stylists are licensed stylists who have completed our 6 month training program and continue their education quarterly. They are building their clientele while working on advanced techniques.

Level 2- 4 Stylist: These stylists are continuing education quarterly and have increased experience in services and have higher clientele demand, earning an increase in price structure with each level.

Level 5-6 Stylist: These Stylists are our most experienced stylists in the EYEFUL Culture. They are mentors in our company and work closely with Student Stylists. They have earned another increased price structure due to their dedication to education, guest experience and high client demand.

When booking an appointment you can choose a stylist that best fits your beauty budget, pricing is listed in the booking process.



CANCELLATION POLICY


As our stylists are in high demand, EYEFUL Beauty requires a deposit to reserve your appointment. We understand some circumstances cannot be avoided, and we are happy to cancel or reschedule your appointment with 24 hours notice. If you wish to cancel your appointment without rescheduling and there is more than 24 hours notice, we will refund your deposit automatically.

Please note that cancellations or no-shows within 24 hours of your appointment will result in a non-refundable deposit. This policy includes both scheduled no-shows and appointments made within 24 hours.

Please be aware: if you are more than 15 minutes late for your scheduled reservation, we cannot guarantee that you will receive all services scheduled to be performed by the stylist. Our team will do our best to accommodate your needs.

If you struggle to find your perfect time and date, please get in touch with the salon so we may add you to the waiting list.
